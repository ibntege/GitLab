Risotto is a dish from northern Italy made with rice and stock ( a liquid flavored with ingredients such as vegetables or chicken). The most important ingredient is good quality risotto rice. Ideally canaroli or vialone nano, both of which are available from good food shops. When cooked, this rice has a frim texture as well as a creamy softness.
